      The Education iBot serves students in need of a personalized tutoring experience. One of the core principles of Artificial Intelligence is repetitive learning and discovery through data. Trained with thousands of math problems, ranging from elementary math up to college-level problem sets, the iBot will expedite your child's learning process by providing them with a world-class instructor at the touch of their finger-tips.

      The Education iBot is equipped with highly intuitive and utile parental controls, allowing teachers or guidance councelors to set any necessary restrictions on their child's growth and development. It should be stressed that the Education iBot is intended for use alongside teacher oversight, not as a supplement to it.

      Taking after the principle of adapting to changes in its surrounding enviornment, the iBot system is decked out with software to detect when a system update is needed, allowing the programming to be continually adjusted as deemed fit by the trusted developers at the ML Solutions offices.

      The iBot is constantly undergoing the data-collection process. By signing up to purchase an iBot, you can be a part of a wider analysis of child learning habits.
